To add your actual FNP logo:
1. Save your FNP logo as "fnp-favicon.png" (32x32 pixels) in the public/static/ folder
2. Or convert the SVG to PNG using an online converter
3. The favicon will automatically appear in the browser tab

Current setup uses:
- SVG favicon (modern browsers)
- PNG fallback (older browsers)
- Apple touch icon (iOS devices)
